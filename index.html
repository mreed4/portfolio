About

I am a millenial California native born and raised in the the Bay Area though I have spent the last twenty years in San Diego.

I have a fiancée—Brittni—and a little boy on the way! I enjoy music, good beer, cooking, and my single-speed bicycle. I reject virtually all forms of social media.

Right now I use HTML, CSS, and small amounts of vanilla Javascript. One of my goals is to develop a Javascript powered Markdown based blog platform, ideally using Express for the backend. Codecademy Pro, The Odin Project, Free Code Camp, and many other resources are providing me with the knowledge and education so far.
