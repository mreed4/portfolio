This is the about me section

I I have dabbled off-and-on in web development for about twenty years now, purely at a hobbyist level. My first memory of development is in 1999 or so for a personal website about the computer game Counter-Strike.

In terms of my style or the over-arching themes of my work, I tend to appreciate minimalism [but not ultra-minimalism], negative space, typography-forward designs, subtle animations, and simple color palettes. "Less is more" and what not.

On a more personal level, I am a millenial California native born and raised in the the Bay Area, though I have spent twenty years now in San Diego. I have a fiancee—Brittni—and a baby on the way. I enjoy coding, music, good beer, my single speed bicycle [it's not a fixie, I swear], and computer games on occasion.
