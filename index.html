04

#Flexco

Here the project was to design a company page, using CSS flexbox. Similar to the club project, the company could be imagined or real.

Check it out Live. Browse the Repo. If you have a Codecademy account, check out the Project page or the forum category about the project.

Notes

I used both grid and flex. I also got a little "meta" with this with the name of the company. Ideally I wanted it to look "corporate", but not boring. Initially I based it around an off-red monochrome color scheme, but that just didn't or couldn't work with what I wanted to do with the Tier cards. All of the images are from Pexels I believe. It should be but is not yet responsive. I have yet to go in and add the smooth-scroll to jump around the page. The social media icons at the bottom have a hover state!
